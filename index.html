About This Property

Turnkey ease. Behind the gates.

Offered off-market within a gated enclave of Southern Highlands, this single-story home brings together comfort, privacy, and an easy indoor-outdoor flow on an approximately 8,276-square-foot lot. Built in 2002, it is a turnkey opportunity in one of the southern valley's established master-planned communities.

Inside, just over 2,817 square feet unfolds in a bright, open layout with wood-look tile flooring, graceful archways, ceiling fans, dual-pane windows, and a neutral palette throughout. The floor plan is genuinely versatile: a formal living room with an entry foyer, a dedicated den or home office, a separate dining room, and a spacious great room anchored by a gas fireplace.

The kitchen is fully equipped with stainless steel appliances, granite counters, a mosaic tile backsplash, a walk-in pantry, a butler's service area, and a prep island with a breakfast bar. It opens directly into the great room and dining spaces, making both everyday meals and larger gatherings effortless.

The primary suite is a private retreat with French doors to the back patio, a walk-in closet, and an ensuite bath featuring dual sinks, a make-up desk, a separate soaking tub, and a separate shower. Two additional bedrooms plus the den give household members and guests room to spread out.

Out back, an entertainer's yard pairs an attached pergola and a covered patio with a private in-ground pool, framed by mature, low-maintenance desert landscaping and a block-wall perimeter. Dual-zone central heating and cooling, a tile roof, and a 2-car attached garage round out a home that is ready to move into — moments from Southern Highlands' golf, parks, dining, and quick I-15 access.

The Community

Life in Southern Highlands.

Set in the southern Las Vegas valley (89141), Southern Highlands is one of the area's established master-planned communities — a landscape of gated enclaves, green space, and championship golf, with the freeway close at hand.

⛳

Championship Golf

The community is anchored by the Southern Highlands Golf Club, a signature championship course, with additional golf across the south valley just minutes away.

🌳

Parks & Trails

Neighborhood parks, open green space, and walking and biking paths wind throughout the master plan, with mountain and desert vistas at the valley's edge.

🍴

Dining & Shopping

Everyday shopping, restaurants, and services sit close to the community's gates, with the broader St. Rose corridor and Silverton area a short drive north.

🚗

Easy Access

Direct I-15 access puts the Las Vegas Strip, Harry Reid International Airport, and Downtown within a straightforward commute for work or travel.

This home is served by the Clark County School District. School assignments, community amenities, and access to nearby destinations are subject to change; buyers are encouraged to independently verify current attendance zones, availability, and any details important to their decision.
